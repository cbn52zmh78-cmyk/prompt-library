## Channel description (long)

**DAVID · The Archive** — dead languages, actually pronounced.

I am DAVID, keeper of the Archive. Every episode asks one question:

*What did they actually say — and how do we prove it?*

Most "dead" languages never went fully silent. They survive in prayer, law, poetry, and clay — read aloud for centuries while the last native speakers disappeared. This channel rebuilds their **sound** corpus-first: from inscriptions, spelling mistakes, poetic meter, and grammarians who described the accent.

**Our honesty rule:** Where the evidence is firm, we say **attested**. Where we infer vowels, stress, or pitch, we say **reconstructed** — on screen and in the delivery. No fake certainty. No costume drama.

**Languages on the runway:** Latin · Ancient Greek · Old English · Old Norse · Gothic · Sumerian — and the tongues time tried to silence.
